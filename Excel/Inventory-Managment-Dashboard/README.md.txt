## Inventory Management Dashboard

### Business Objective
To track inventory levels, identify low-stock items, and calculate reorder quantities
to prevent stockouts and optimize supplier ordering.

### Dataset
Inventory data including:
- Item Code & Name
- Supplier
- Unit Cost
- Stock Quantity
- Reorder Point
- Lead Time

### Key KPIs
- Total SKUs
- Low Stock Items
- Out of Stock Items
- Average Lead Time
- Total Reorder Cost

### Key Visuals
- Stock Quantity vs Reorder Point
- Low Stock Items by Product
- Reorder Cost by Supplier
- Lead Time Comparison

### Tools & Techniques
- Excel formulas (IF, LOOKUP, SUMIFS)
- Conditional formatting
- Pivot Tables
- Inventory dashboard design

### Outcome
The dashboard supports inventory planning by highlighting critical items,
supplier performance, and total reorder cost.
