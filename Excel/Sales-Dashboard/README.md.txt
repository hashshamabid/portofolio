## Sales Analysis Dashboard

### Business Objective
To analyze sales performance across time, regions, products, and sales representatives
in order to identify trends, top performers, and revenue drivers.

### Dataset
Sales transaction data including:
- Date (Year & Month)
- Region
- Product
- Sales Representative
- Quantity Sold
- Unit Price
- Revenue

### Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Units Sold
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
- Top Customer
- Top Salesperson

### Key Visuals
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Sales Representative

### Tools & Techniques
- Pivot Tables
- Slicers (Year, Product, Region)
- Calculated fields
- Interactive Excel dashboard design

### Outcome
The dashboard provides management with a clear overview of sales performance,
enabling quick identification of growth trends, top contributors, and underperforming areas.
