## Financial Forecast Model (Scenario Analysis)

### Business Objective
To forecast future revenue, expenses, net profit, and cash balance
under different business scenarios.

### Dataset
- Historical financial data
- Assumptions for growth rates and costs
- Scenario controls (Base, Best, Worst case)

### Key Features
- Scenario selector
- Dynamic growth rates
- Automated 12-month forecast
- Cash balance projection

### Key KPIs
- Forecasted Revenue
- Forecasted Expenses
- Net Profit
- Cash Balance
- Scenario Impact

### Tools & Techniques
- Excel forecasting logic
- Scenario-based assumptions
- Structured financial modeling
- Dashboard-ready outputs

### Outcome
The model allows stakeholders to evaluate future financial performance
and assess risk under different scenarios.
