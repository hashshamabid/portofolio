## HR Employee Tracker Dashboard

### Business Objective
To monitor workforce metrics such as headcount, salary, attendance, performance,
and employee turnover for HR decision-making.

### Dataset
Employee-level HR data including:
- Department & Role
- Salary
- Join Date
- Performance Score
- Days Present & Absent
- Employment Status (Active / Leaver)

### Key KPIs
- Total Employees
- Average Salary
- Average Tenure
- New Hires
- Turnover Rate
- Attendance Rate

### Key Visuals
- Employee Count by Department
- Average Performance Score
- Average Salary by Department
- Average Absent Days

### Tools & Techniques
- Excel formulas (DATEDIF, IF, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS)
- Pivot Tables
- Interactive filters and search by Employee ID
- KPI cards and dashboards

### Outcome
This tracker helps HR teams monitor workforce health, identify attendance issues,
analyze compensation trends, and track employee turnover effectively.
